# Frequently Asked Questions

## Orders

### Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?

You can cancel your order while it has **not yet shipped**. Once an order has shipped, it cannot be cancelled — you can return it for a refund once it arrives using our standard return policy. To request a cancellation, contact support immediately with your order number.

### Can I add items to an existing order?

No. Once an order is placed, items cannot be added to it. If you forgot something, place a separate order. If both orders ship together, you will not be charged extra shipping.

### Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes. Gift wrapping is available as an add-on at checkout for **$4.99 per order**. You can include a personalized gift message (up to 150 characters) at no additional cost. Gift-wrapped orders are packed in branded tissue paper inside a gift box with a ribbon. The packing slip included will not show prices.

### Are your products authentic and genuine?

Yes. We source all products directly from authorized manufacturers or their official distributors. We do not sell third-party marketplace items, grey-market goods, or refurbished products unless explicitly labeled as such. If you have concerns about a specific product's authenticity, contact support with your order number.

### What happens if my package is stolen after delivery?

We are sorry to hear that. Here is what to do:

1. File a police report and obtain the report number.
2. Contact our support team with your order number, delivery confirmation details, and the police report number.
3. We will file a carrier claim on your behalf.
4. Resolution typically takes 5–7 business days. We may reship the order or issue a refund depending on the outcome of the carrier investigation.

We are unable to process stolen package claims without a police report.

### Can I purchase in bulk for my business?

Yes. We offer bulk pricing for qualifying business orders. Contact our support team with the items and quantities you need. Bulk orders typically require a minimum of 10 units per SKU. A dedicated account manager will be assigned for orders over $1,000.

## Payments and Pricing

### How do I apply a promo code?

Enter your promo code in the **"Discount Code"** field on the checkout page, then click "Apply." The discount will be reflected in your order total before you confirm payment. Only one promo code can be applied per order. Promo codes cannot be applied after an order is placed.

### Why was I charged a different amount than shown at checkout?

The most common reasons for a discrepancy:

- **Sales tax** — Tax is calculated based on your shipping address and is shown in the order summary at checkout.
- **Currency conversion** — If your bank account is in a non-USD currency, your bank applies its own conversion rate and may add a foreign transaction fee.
- **Pre-authorization hold** — Some banks show a temporary authorization hold that differs from the final charge. The final charge reflects the actual order total.

If none of these explain the difference, contact support with your order number and bank statement detail.

### Do you have a loyalty or rewards program?

Not yet, but it is on our roadmap. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when our loyalty program launches. We plan to offer points on every purchase redeemable for store credit.

### Can I get a receipt for tax purposes?

Yes. An itemized receipt is emailed automatically when your order ships. You can also download a PDF invoice from your account's Order History at any time. If you need a formal invoice with additional business details, contact support.

## Returns and Refunds

### How long does it take to receive my refund?

Once we receive and inspect your return (1–3 business days after delivery to our warehouse), refunds are processed within **3–5 business days** for credit/debit cards, and **1–3 business days** for PayPal. You will receive an email confirmation when the refund is issued. Total time from return delivery to money in your account is typically 5–8 business days.

### My refund never arrived. What should I do?

First, check the email confirmation we sent when the refund was issued — it includes the exact amount and the payment method it was returned to. Then check with your bank, as processing times vary. If more than 10 business days have passed since the refund confirmation email, contact support and we will investigate.

### Can I return a gift?

Yes. Gift recipients can return items for store credit. We do not require the original purchaser to initiate the return. Contact support with the order number (found on the packing slip) and we will generate a return label for you.

## Shipping

### Do you ship to US territories (Puerto Rico, Guam, etc.)?

Yes, we ship to all US territories. Standard shipping rates apply. Express and Overnight shipping are not available to US territories. Delivery times may be longer than standard mainland US estimates.

### My tracking hasn't updated in several days. Is my package lost?

Tracking updates can sometimes pause for 2–3 days during transit, especially during high-volume periods. This is normal. If tracking has not updated for **7 or more business days** and the estimated delivery date has passed, contact support and we will investigate with the carrier.

## Account and Privacy

### How is my personal data used?

Your personal data (name, email, address, purchase history) is used solely to process your orders, provide customer support, and send you communications you have opted into. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. For full details, see our Privacy Policy. We are GDPR compliant for customers in the European Economic Area.

### How do I stop receiving marketing emails?

Click "Unsubscribe" at the bottom of any marketing email, or go to Account Settings > Notifications and disable promotional emails. Unsubscribing takes effect within 48 hours. You will still receive transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping updates) as these are required for your orders.
