# Payment Methods and Billing

We accept a wide range of payment options to make checkout as convenient as possible.

## Accepted Payment Methods

### Credit and Debit Cards
We accept all major credit and debit cards:
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Discover

Cards must be issued by a US or international bank and have a valid billing address on file.

### Digital Wallets
- **PayPal** — Link your PayPal account for fast, secure checkout.
- **Apple Pay** — Available on Safari and iOS devices.
- **Google Pay** — Available on Chrome and Android devices.

### Store Credit and Gift Cards
Store credit and gift cards issued by our store can be applied at checkout. Gift cards can be purchased in denominations of $25, $50, $100, and $200.

## When You Are Charged

We do not charge your payment method at the time you place your order. Your card is charged only **when your order ships**. If an item in your order is backordered or ships separately, you will only be charged for each portion when it ships.

Pre-authorization holds may appear on your bank statement at the time of order, but these are released if the order is cancelled or adjusted before shipment.

## Receipts and Invoices

An email receipt is automatically sent to the email address on file within **1 hour of shipment**. The receipt includes an itemized breakdown of products, taxes, shipping, and any discounts applied.

If you need a duplicate receipt or a formal invoice (e.g., for business expense reporting), contact support with your order number and we will email it to you within one business day.

## Failed Payments

If your payment fails at checkout, common causes include:

- Incorrect billing address (must match the address on file with your bank)
- Insufficient funds or credit limit
- Card flagged for unusual activity by your bank
- Expired card

If your payment fails, try the following steps:
1. Double-check the card number, expiration date, and CVV.
2. Ensure your billing address matches exactly what your bank has on file.
3. Contact your bank to check for blocks on the transaction.
4. Try a different payment method.

If an order was placed and payment later fails (e.g., during a pre-authorization capture at shipment), we will email you to update your payment details. The order will be held for **48 hours** before being cancelled.

## Promo Codes and Discounts

Promo codes can be entered in the "Discount Code" field at checkout. Rules for promo codes:

- **One code per order** — multiple promo codes cannot be stacked.
- Promo codes cannot be combined with other offers unless explicitly stated.
- Codes are case-insensitive.
- Expired or invalid codes will show an error message at checkout.
- Promo codes cannot be applied retroactively to completed orders.

## Price Matching

We offer price matching within **14 days of your purchase** if you find the identical item at a lower price on a competitor's website. To request a price match:

- The item must be the same model, size, and color.
- The competitor must have the item in stock at the time of your request.
- The price must be from an authorized retailer (not a marketplace seller or auction site).
- Contact support with a link to the competitor's listing and your order number.

Approved price matches are refunded as store credit or back to your original payment method.

## Split Payments

We do not currently support splitting a single order across multiple payment methods. However, you can apply store credit or a gift card in combination with one other payment method.

## Sales Tax

Sales tax is calculated automatically at checkout based on your shipping address and applicable state tax laws. Tax rates vary by state and by product category. The final tax amount is shown before you confirm your order.

We collect and remit sales tax in all states that require it. If you are a tax-exempt organization, contact support before placing your order to set up a tax-exempt account.

## Currency

All prices are displayed and charged in **US Dollars (USD)**. If you are purchasing from outside the United States, your bank or payment provider will handle currency conversion at their current exchange rate. We are not responsible for foreign transaction fees.

## Security

All payment information is encrypted using TLS and processed through PCI-DSS compliant payment processors. We do not store your full card number on our servers. Saved payment methods are stored as secure tokens by our payment processor.
