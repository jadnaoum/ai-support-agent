# Account Management

Your account gives you access to order history, saved addresses, payment methods, and preferences. Here's everything you need to manage your account.

## Creating an Account

You can create an account at any time by clicking "Sign Up" and providing your name, email address, and a password. You can also create an account during checkout by checking the "Save my information" option.

Accounts can also be created using social login via **Google** or **Apple**. Social login accounts are linked to your email address — if you already have an account with the same email, the social login will be connected to your existing account.

## Password Reset

If you have forgotten your password:

1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page.
2. Enter the email address associated with your account.
3. You will receive a password reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it does not arrive.
4. The reset link is valid for **24 hours**. After it expires, you will need to request a new one.
5. Create a new password that is at least 8 characters long and includes a mix of letters and numbers.

For security, we do not display your current password and cannot retrieve it — only reset it.

## Changing Your Email Address

To change the email address on your account:

1. Log in and go to Account Settings.
2. Click "Edit" next to your email address.
3. Enter your current password to confirm your identity.
4. Enter the new email address.
5. A verification link will be sent to the **new email address**. You must click this link to complete the change.

Until the new email is verified, your account continues to use the old address. Verification links expire after 48 hours.

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security to your account. When enabled, you will be asked for a one-time code in addition to your password each time you log in.

To enable 2FA:
1. Go to Account Settings > Security.
2. Choose between **SMS verification** (code sent to your phone) or an **Authenticator app** (such as Google Authenticator or Authy).
3. Follow the setup instructions to link your phone or app.

We strongly recommend enabling 2FA, especially if your account has saved payment methods.

## Managing Saved Addresses

You can save multiple shipping addresses to your account for faster checkout. To add, edit, or delete a saved address, go to Account Settings > Addresses.

You can designate one address as your **default shipping address**, which will be pre-filled at checkout.

## Saved Payment Methods

Payment methods can be saved to your account for faster checkout. We store payment methods as encrypted tokens through our payment processor — your full card number is never stored on our servers.

To manage saved payment methods, go to Account Settings > Payment Methods. You can add new cards, remove existing ones, or set a default payment method.

## Order History

Your complete order history is available in the Orders section of your account. For each order you can:

- View the itemized receipt
- Track shipment status
- Start a return
- Download a PDF invoice
- Re-order the same items

Order history is retained indefinitely for active accounts.

## Notification Preferences

You can control which email and SMS notifications you receive. Go to Account Settings > Notifications to manage your preferences.

Available notification types:
- **Order confirmations** — sent when you place an order (cannot be disabled)
- **Shipping updates** — tracking numbers and delivery confirmations
- **Promotional emails** — sale announcements, new arrivals, and discount codes
- **Product back-in-stock alerts** — notified when a saved item is available
- **CSAT surveys** — satisfaction survey after order delivery

To unsubscribe from all marketing emails, click "Unsubscribe" at the bottom of any marketing email, or disable promotional emails in your account settings.

## Linking and Unlinking Social Login

If you want to link a social login (Google or Apple) to your existing account, go to Account Settings > Security > Connected Accounts. You can also unlink a social login from this page, provided you have a password set on your account (so you are not locked out).

## Account Deletion

To permanently delete your account, contact our support team. Before deletion:

- You will be asked to confirm your identity.
- A **30-day grace period** applies — your account is deactivated but not deleted, allowing you to cancel the request.
- Any pending orders will still be processed and fulfilled.
- Refunds and returns for orders placed before deletion will still be honored.
- After 30 days, your account and personal data will be permanently deleted in accordance with our privacy policy.

Account deletion is irreversible after the grace period ends. Order history cannot be recovered after deletion.

## Accessibility Settings

Our website supports standard browser accessibility features. If you need accommodations or encounter accessibility issues, contact our support team and we will assist you directly.
